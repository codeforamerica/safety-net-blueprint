ORCA: Object-Relationship Content Attributes

ORCA is a methodology for organizing and presenting data models in a way that's intuitive for designers. This reference documents the Safety Net API data model—the standardized fields, types, and relationships used across benefit program applications. Use this reference to understand what data exists, how it's structured, and what values are valid for each field.

Data Domains

The data model is organized into these domains:

  • Client Management — Persistent client identity & history
  • Intake — Application submission & intake processing
  • Eligibility — Program eligibility determination
  • Case Management — Cases, workers & staff assignments
  • Document Management — Files, uploads & verification
  • Workflow — Task lifecycle, queues & SLA tracking
  • Data Exchange — External service facade — FDSH, IEVS, SAVE, SSA
  • Scheduling — Appointments & interviews
  • Communications — Notices, letters & client alerts
  • Appeals & Hearings — Fair hearings & appeal decisions
  • Benefits & Payments — Benefit issuance, EBT & recoupment
