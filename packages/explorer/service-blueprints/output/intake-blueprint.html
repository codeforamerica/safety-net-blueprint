Regulations
SNAP: 30-day processing clock
POLICY
7 CFR § 273.2(g) — Clock starts at submission; determination must be completed within 30 days (7 days for expedited applications).
Expedited SNAP: 7-day clock
POLICY
7 CFR § 273.2(i) — If expedited screening at submission returns positive, a 7-day processing deadline applies instead of the standard 30-day deadline. Expedited criteria include low income, low resources, or migrant/seasonal farm worker status.
Medicaid: 45-day processing clock starts at submission
POLICY
42 CFR § 435.912 — Determination must be completed within 45 days. 90 days if disability determination required — indicated by program type (e.g., Medicaid ABD vs. MAGI Medicaid).
SNAP: interview required before determination
POLICY
7 CFR § 273.2(e) — May be waived by state policy

POLICY
